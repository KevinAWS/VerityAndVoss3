OUR MISSION

We thrive on bringing innovative high quality products to the market, which in turn are supported by a world class team from sales through to distribution.

Our mission is to bring quality pet products to the market that will enhance both the pets life and the owners. Our vision is simple; to reflect the love of your pet through product quality, innovation and style.

OUR BRANDS

Both Oggi’s Oven and TRIKKA are pet industry “firsts” to market.

We continue to invest resources in extending these ranges by designing innovative products which will aid the quality of both the Pet and owners life.

OUR PARTNERS

We are proud to work closely with our product partners, Kiwi Walker, Max & Lucy and Magisso.

All partners share our own core values of: loyal, Trustful, Respectful and honourable.
