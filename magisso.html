Magisso

Happy Pet Project is a brand founded in 2018 by the awarded Finnish design Lab - Magisso. Using their celebrated design skills ( over 30 design awards ) to capture the revolutionary self-cooling ceramic technology they have created a breath taking collection of pet ceramics including, slow feeding bowls, water bowls and treat jars. Not only would these ceramics make a elegant addition to any environment, these products ensure pleasant and safe refreshments for your pet at all times.
