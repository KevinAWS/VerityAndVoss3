Trikka

A real pet industry FIRST! A high quality training clicker which has been encapsulated in a handy palm sized case to allow for treats to be added and be dispensed in one operation!

Designed to have 3 functions the unique "split button" design of the TRIKKA allows the trainer/pet owner to use the clicker ONLY, click AND dispense a treat simultaneously, or, to just dispense a treat! Complimented by a range of TRIKKA Training Treats in 250g refill pouches with further flavours and products already in development this really is the brand to watch!

Launched in 2016, the TRIKKA was honoured to be awarded "Highly Commended" in the "Pet Product Innovation of the year" awards.
